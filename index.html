Cal Poly Connections

Nuestra Ciencia is a multidisciplinary research program developed through a collaboration between the Liberal Studies and Biology departments at Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. Since its launch in 2021, the program has fostered a strong, ongoing partnership between the university and the local community, growing steadily in both reach and impact. Nuestra Ciencia es un programa de investigación multidisciplinario desarrollado a través de una colaboración entre los departamentos de Estudios Liberales y Biología en Cal Poly, San Luis Obispo. Desde su lanzamiento en 2021, el programa ha fomentado una sólida y continua asociación entre la universidad y la comunidad local, creciendo constantemente en alcance e impacto.

Our Students

Nuestra Ciencia focuses on the impact of bilingual microbiology education on both K-12 students and Cal Poly undergraduates. This initiative creates a unique opportunity to connect undergraduate students as mentors for Latinx youth, fostering a supportive peer-to-peer environment where all students can learn, grow, and succeed. Nuestra Ciencia se enfoca en el impacto de la enseñanza bilingüe de microbiología tanto en estudiantes de K-12 como en estudiantes universitarios de Cal Poly. Esta iniciativa crea una oportunidad única para conectar a los estudiantes universitarios como mentores de jóvenes latinos, fomentando un ambiente de apoyo entre pares donde todos los estudiantes, puedan aprender, crecer y tener éxito.

Bringing Microbiology to Bilingual Classrooms

Changing the world by eliminating microbiology misconceptions... one student at a time.

Lessons on Food Safety make students more confident when washing, chilling, separating, and cooking food.

Teaching about Disease Transmission and Prevention equips students with the knowledge of how to protect themselves and others by getting vaccinated.

By underlining the importance of good Hand Washing, students can better understand how to prevent the spread of germs and bacteria.
