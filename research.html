Research

Nuestra Ciencia’s research asks big questions about identity, language, and science learning. Our work centers community voices and challenges traditional ideas of who belongs in science.

Our Team (Students)

If you’re interested in connecting with one of our student-led teams, we’d love to hear from you! Whether you want to collaborate with our Outreach Team to build community partnerships, work with our Research Team to study educational impact, or engage with our Lesson Development Team to help create dynamic bilingual science lessons, there’s a place for you at Nuestra Ciencia. Reach out — we’re excited to collaborate and grow together!

Research and Lesson Development: nuestracienciacp@gmail.com
Outreach: @nuestra.ciencia

Our Leaders (Faculty)

If you would like to connect with our faculty leaders, Dr. Yep, Dr. Nation, or Dr. Ramos Carranza, we welcome your questions and ideas! Our advisors bring years of experience in science education, bilingual outreach, and research, and they are always happy to support collaboration, mentorship, and new opportunities that strengthen Nuestra Ciencia’s mission. Please don't hesitate to reach out — they look forward to hearing from you!
