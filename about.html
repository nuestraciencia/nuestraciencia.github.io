About Us

We are empowering the next generation through inclusive, hands-on science led by undergraduate students.

Nuestra Ciencia is a bilingual, community-driven research and outreach program connecting Cal Poly undergraduates with local elementary students through microbiology education. Born from a partnership between Cal Poly’s Liberal Studies and Biology departments, Nuestra Ciencia was created to dismantle misconceptions in microbiology and to empower Latinx youth by showing them that science is for everyone. From 2021-24, NC has provided opportunities for over 50 undergraduate students to gain experience teaching bilingual microbiology lessons, and for over 300 2nd-6th grade students from five local bilingual schools to experience science activities in Spanish. Undergraduate mentors design and teach relevant lessons on topics like vaccines and food safety, helping children not only grasp scientific concepts but also see themselves as future scientists. At the same time, college students gain invaluable experience teaching in Spanish, rediscovering the value of their language, culture, and voice in STEM. Through our Aprender Haciendo Lab ('Learn By Doing Lab'), we bring elementary students to the Cal Poly campus to learn in real labs and spark a lasting interest in science. Nuestra Ciencia is more than a program—it’s a growing movement to transform how science is taught, who it includes, and what it means to belong.

Our Teams

Nuestra Ciencia is composed of three teams — Research, Lesson Development, and Outreach — to study impact, create engaging science lessons, and build lasting community connections.

Our Research Team investigates the impact of bilingual microbiology education on both elementary students and Cal Poly undergraduates. Using a participatory, design-based research approach, the team collects and analyzes data—such as surveys, interviews, and teaching artifacts—to better understand how students’ views on science, identity, and language evolve. Their work helps refine lesson design and provides valuable insight into how bilingual education fosters empowerment, belonging, and STEM engagement across communities.

Our Lesson Development Team creates fun, interactive science lessons that connect with students' lives and cultures. From hands-on activities to real-life examples, these lessons involve K-12 students in Cal Poly's signature Learn-By-Doing teaching philosophy. The team works collaboratively to ensure each lesson is educational, culturally relevant, and engaging for young learners.

Our Outreach Team helps tell the story of Nuestra Ciencia through eye-catching posters, flyers, and social media. They're the creative minds behind our visual style — from designing our logo and brand colors to building this website! Whether it’s spreading the word about events or making science feel more fun and welcoming, this team makes sure everything we share looks great and connects with our community.
